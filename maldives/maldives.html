Getting Around the Maldives

Efficient and scenic ways to explore the Maldives:

  • Seaplane: A stunning way to travel between islands.
  • Boat: Enjoy the picturesque waters of the Maldives with ferry rides.
  • Local Dhoni: Traditional wooden boats for short distances between islands.

Travel Tips

Maximize your Maldives experience:

  • Book water activities like snorkeling and diving in advance.
  • Respect local culture by dressing modestly on local islands.
  • Visit during the dry season for the best weather.
