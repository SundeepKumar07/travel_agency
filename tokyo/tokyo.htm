Hotels in Tokyo

Explore the best places to stay in Tokyo, whether you're looking for luxury, boutique charm, or budget-friendly options.

  • Luxury: The Peninsula Tokyo, Park Hyatt Tokyo
  • Boutique: Claska, Hotel Niwa Tokyo
  • Budget: Khaosan Tokyo, Sakura Hostel Asakusa

Famous Landmarks

Discover iconic attractions that make Tokyo unforgettable:

  • Tokyo Tower: A symbol of Tokyo with panoramic city views.
  • Meiji Shrine: A serene Shinto shrine surrounded by forest.
  • Asakusa Temple: Tokyo's oldest temple, home to the iconic Kaminarimon gate.
  • Shibuya Crossing: The world's busiest pedestrian crossing, a true Tokyo experience.

Getting Around Tokyo

Efficient and convenient ways to explore Tokyo:

  • Metro: The Tokyo Metro is the fastest and most convenient way to navigate the city.
  • JR Trains: The Japan Rail system connects Tokyo to neighboring cities and regions.
  • Taxis: A comfortable option, though it can be expensive in comparison to trains and buses.

Travel Tips

Maximize your Tokyo experience:

  • Get a Suica card for easy access to trains, buses, and vending machines.
  • Learn basic Japanese phrases like "Arigatou" (thank you) and "Sumimasen" (excuse me).
  • Visit during spring or fall for the most pleasant weather and beautiful scenery.
