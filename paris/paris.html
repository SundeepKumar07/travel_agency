Hotels in Paris

Explore the best places to stay in Paris, whether you're looking for luxury, boutique charm, or budget-friendly options.

  • Luxury: The Ritz Paris, Shangri-La Hotel
  • Boutique: Hotel Providence, Le Narcisse Blanc
  • Budget: Ibis Paris Montmartre, Generator Paris

Famous Landmarks

Discover iconic attractions that make Paris unforgettable:

  • Eiffel Tower: The symbol of Paris, offering stunning city views.
  • Louvre Museum: Home to the Mona Lisa and countless masterpieces.
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral: A Gothic architectural marvel.
  • Palace of Versailles: A majestic royal residence with breathtaking gardens.

Getting Around Paris

Efficient and convenient ways to explore Paris:

  • Metro: The fastest way to navigate the city.
  • Bus: Enjoy scenic routes through Paris neighborhoods.
  • Bike Rentals: Explore at your own pace with Vélib' Métropole.

Travel Tips

Maximize your Paris experience:

  • Buy skip-the-line tickets for major attractions.
  • Use a Paris Museum Pass to save time and money.
  • Visit during off-peak seasons for fewer crowds.
